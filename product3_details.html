    Limitations Faced by Drone Operators


    Endurance


    • Commercial drones have an average battery life of 20-30 minutes. Which limits the flight time and means constant battery changes.

    Reliability


    • Drone accidents are mainly linked to signal losses, interference, fly-away or battery issues.

    Legislation


    • In many countries a tether is seen as a positive mitigation to operate in areas that would normally be restrictive or sensitive for drone ops.
